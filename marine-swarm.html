Field Manual

COMMAND — Hold & drag anywhere. Your entire force flows to your finger, swarm-style. Release and they hold position.

BEACON PADS — Stand on a pad to capture it. Owned pads mine minerals and deploy fresh marines automatically. The gold CORE pad mines double and deploys faster.

GRID MAPS — On the GRID skirmish map the whole field is tiled into cells. Stand on any cell to paint it your color; owned cells mine and reinforce like small pads. Erase every enemy cell to win.

UPGRADES — Spend minerals in the bottom tray (keys 1–7): damage, fire rate, speed, range, armor, reinforce speed, supply cap.

STIMPACK — Unlock the combat stim (key 8), then fire it with 8 or SPACE: every marine trades a slice of health (never fatal) for a burst of fire rate and speed.

ABSORB — Surround isolated enemies. Marines cut off and outnumbered may defect to your side when they fall.

ENDLESS — Hold the sector against ever-bigger Horde waves. Each wave is announced with its direction — retake pads and buy upgrades in the lull. The Horde takes ground only to starve you; survive as many waves as you can.

GAUNTLET — A run of eight battles, each rung harder than the last. Win a rung, draft one of three perks — they last the whole run. Lose once, or walk out of a fight, and the run is over. Clear the finale to conquer the Gauntlet.

DEFEAT — Lose every marine and every pad and you're out. Same goes for them.
